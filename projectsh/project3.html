Overview

The Ad Hoc Queries project is a comprehensive collection of ad hoc queries designed to extract specific information from a database. Ad hoc queries are dynamic queries that are created on-the-fly to fulfill immediate information needs, as opposed to pre-defined or pre-built queries.

This project serves as a valuable resource for developers, data analysts, and database administrators who often encounter situations where standard queries are insufficient to retrieve the required data. By providing a repository of pre-defined ad hoc queries, the project aims to save time and effort by offering readily available query templates that can be adapted and modified to suit specific database schemas and information needs.
